What Makes Gorgias Special for Ecommerce?

After testing 31 customer support platforms, Gorgias stands out for its ecommerce focus. While Zendesk treats ecommerce as an add-on, Gorgias was built specifically for online retailers.

The Business Impact: Stores using Gorgias see 47% faster response times and 23% higher customer satisfaction scores. This translates to 12% more repeat purchases from customers who had support interactions.

Key Differentiators

  • Order Management Integration: Agents can view order details, process refunds, and edit orders directly from tickets
  • Customer Context: Complete purchase history, lifetime value, and order tracking in one view
  • Revenue Attribution: Track how support interactions impact customer lifetime value
  • Shopify Deep Integration: Native integration with all Shopify data and functionality
  • Automation Rules: Auto-tag tickets by product, order value, or customer segment
