Evaluation Criteria

  • ROI Performance: Revenue generated per dollar spent on the platform
  • Shopify Integration: Depth of ecommerce features and data sync quality
  • Ease of Use: Setup complexity and daily management requirements
  • Automation Capabilities: Workflow sophistication and pre-built templates
  • Deliverability: Inbox placement rates and email performance
  • Support Quality: Response times and solution effectiveness
  • Value for Money: Features provided relative to pricing

Choosing the Right Platform for Your Store

If You're Just Starting ($0-5K/month revenue):

Choose Mailchimp - The free plan supports 2,000 contacts and provides essential email marketing features. Upgrade to paid plans as you grow.

If You're Growing ($5K-25K/month revenue):

Choose Omnisend - Excellent automation features at reasonable pricing. Perfect balance of functionality and affordability for scaling stores.

If You're Established ($25K+/month revenue):

Choose Klaviyo - The advanced ecommerce features justify the premium pricing for established businesses.

If You Want Maximum Automation:

Choose Omnisend - Best-in-class workflow builder with pre-built ecommerce automation that's easy to implement and highly effective.

Bottom Line: Which Email App to Choose?

For most growing Shopify stores, Klaviyo delivers the best results. Despite higher pricing, the superior ecommerce integration makes it the clear winner for serious online retailers.

Start with your budget and growth stage: Mailchimp for testing and small budgets, Omnisend for balanced features and pricing, Klaviyo for maximum revenue potential.

Our recommendation: Begin with a free trial of your chosen platform. Most stores see ROI within 30-60 days, making the decision to upgrade straightforward.
