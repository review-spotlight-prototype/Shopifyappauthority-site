Step 3: DNS Configuration

Connecting your custom domain to Shopify requires DNS changes. This step is crucial for professional appearance and SEO benefits.

3

DNS Setup Process

In your domain registrar's control panel, point your A record to Shopify's IP addresses and create a CNAME record for www. Shopify provides specific instructions for major registrars.

DNS Records You'll Need:

  • A Record: @ ‚Üí 23.227.38.65
  • A Record: @ ‚Üí 23.227.38.74
  • A Record: @ ‚Üí 23.227.38.66
  • A Record: @ ‚Üí 23.227.38.67
  • CNAME Record: www ‚Üí shops.myshopify.com

‚ö†Ô∏è DNS Propagation Time

DNS changes can take 24-48 hours to propagate globally. Plan your launch timeline accordingly and don't panic if your domain doesn't work immediately after making changes.

Your theme is your store's foundation for conversions. A poor theme choice can cost you 30-50% of potential sales, while a well-optimized theme can double your conversion rates.

Theme Selection Strategy

Forget flashy designs and complex animations. High-converting themes prioritize speed, mobile optimization, and conversion-focused layouts over visual complexity.

Essential Design Elements

Homepage Optimization

Your homepage has 3 seconds to convince visitors to stay. Here's the proven structure that converts:

  1. Hero Section: Clear value proposition + hero product image
  2. Social Proof: Customer reviews, press mentions, or user count
  3. Featured Products: 3-6 bestsellers with clear pricing
  4. Trust Signals: Guarantees, certifications, security badges
  5. About Section: Brief brand story that builds connection

Product Page Optimization

Product pages drive 70% of your conversions. Optimize these elements for maximum sales:

üéØ

High-Converting Product Page Elements

  • Related product recommendations
