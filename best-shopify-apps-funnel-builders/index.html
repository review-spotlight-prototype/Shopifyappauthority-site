Frequently Asked Questions

Which funnel builder is best for Shopify stores?

ClickFunnels is the industry leader with the most Shopify integrations and proven templates. For beginners, Instapage offers easier setup with professional results.

Do I need a funnel builder if I have Shopify?

Yes, Shopify handles transactions but funnel builders create the marketing sequences that convert cold traffic into buyers. They're essential for scaling beyond organic traffic.

What's the difference between landing pages and sales funnels?

Landing pages capture leads, while sales funnels guide prospects through multiple steps from awareness to purchase. Funnels typically convert 3-5x better for complex products.

Can I build funnels without technical skills?

Yes, all modern funnel builders offer drag-and-drop editors. ClickFunnels and Kartra are especially beginner-friendly with pre-built templates for common Shopify use cases.
