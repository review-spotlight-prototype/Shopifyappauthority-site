How to Get Started with Kit

Step 1: Access Kit in Shopify Admin

Navigate to "Marketing" in your Shopify admin dashboard and click on "Create campaign" to access Kit.

Step 2: Choose Your Campaign Type

Select from welcome emails, product announcements, promotions, or newsletters using pre-built templates.

Step 3: Customize Your Design

Use the drag-and-drop editor to customize colors, add your logo, and automatically include product recommendations.

Step 4: Select Your Audience

Target specific customer segments based on purchase history, location, or engagement with your store.

Step 5: Send and Track Results

Send your campaign and monitor performance directly in your Shopify analytics dashboard.
