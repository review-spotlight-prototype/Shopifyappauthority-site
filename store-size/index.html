Not Sure Which Category You Fit?

Store size isn't just about revenue. Consider your team size, technical resources, and growth trajectory when choosing apps.

Quick Assessment:

  • Small Store: Solo entrepreneur or small team (1-3 people), limited budget, just starting email marketing
  • Medium Store: Growing team (3-10 people), proven product-market fit, ready to invest in growth tools
  • Enterprise Store: Large team (10+ people), complex operations, need advanced reporting and integrations
