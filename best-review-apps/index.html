Choosing the Right Review App for Your Store

If You're Just Starting (0-50 orders/month):

Choose Judge.me Free Plan - Supports 50 orders with comprehensive features. Best value for small stores getting started.

If You're Growing (50-500 orders/month):

Choose Judge.me Paid Plan ($15/month) - Excellent features at the best price point. Perfect balance for growing stores.

If You Want Advanced Features (500+ orders/month):

Choose Yotpo - Comprehensive platform with loyalty integration and advanced analytics for serious growth.

If You Have Visual Products:

Choose Stamped.io - Best-in-class photo and video review collection for products that benefit from visual social proof.

If Budget is Extremely Tight:

Choose Shopify Product Reviews - Completely free with basic functionality. Good starting point for very small stores.
