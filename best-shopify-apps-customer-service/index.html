Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right customer service app for my Shopify store?

Consider your team size, budget, support channels needed, and integration requirements. If you're starting out, Freshdesk offers an excellent free plan. Zendesk provides enterprise features for growing stores. For chat-focused support, consider LiveChat or Intercom. Help Scout offers simplicity with power for small to medium teams.

Which customer service apps integrate best with Shopify?

All major customer service platforms offer strong Shopify integrations. Zendesk, Help Scout, Freshdesk, LiveChat, and Intercom all provide dedicated Shopify apps that sync customer data, order information, and purchase history to give agents complete context during support interactions.

Do I need live chat for my Shopify store?

Live chat can significantly improve customer experience and conversion rates by providing immediate assistance during the shopping process. However, it's not essential for all stores. Consider your target audience, time zones, and team availability when deciding whether to implement live chat functionality.

What's the difference between help desk software and live chat tools?

Help desk software (like Zendesk, Help Scout, Freshdesk) focuses on ticket management, email support, and asynchronous communication. Live chat tools (like LiveChat) specialize in real-time conversations. Some platforms like Intercom combine both approaches, while others require separate tools for different support channels.
