Quick Answers About Google Analytics 360

Is Google Analytics 360 worth $150K+ per year?

Only for enterprises with complex attribution needs and massive data volume. If you need BigQuery export, advanced attribution models, and dedicated support, GA360 provides value. Most businesses can use free GA4 effectively.

What's the main difference between GA4 and GA360?

Advanced attribution modeling and BigQuery data export. GA360 offers machine learning attribution, raw data access, higher limits, and enterprise support that free GA4 doesn't provide.

Who actually needs Google Analytics 360?

Large enterprises with 10M+ monthly hits and complex marketing channels. Companies needing custom attribution models, advanced audiences, and raw data export for machine learning and BI tools.

Can I upgrade from GA4 to GA360 later?

Yes, but plan for implementation time and costs. Upgrading requires new tracking setup, data migration planning, and team training on advanced features. Consider 2-3 month implementation timeline.

Enterprise Features That Drive Advanced Insights

Advanced Attribution Models

Data-driven attribution using machine learning to assign credit across touchpoints. Custom models beyond first-click, last-click, and linear attribution.

BigQuery Data Export

Raw, unsampled data export to BigQuery for custom analysis, machine learning, and integration with business intelligence tools.

Advanced Audiences

Sophisticated audience creation with longer lookback windows and more complex criteria for precise targeting and analysis.

Unsampled Reporting

Access to unsampled data in standard reports with higher thresholds before sampling kicks in for more accurate insights.

Data-Driven Attribution

Machine learning algorithms analyze conversion paths to determine the true impact of each marketing touchpoint automatically.

Enterprise Support

Dedicated technical account manager, priority support, and access to Google analytics consultants for implementation and optimization.

Decision Framework: Is GA360 Right for Your Enterprise?

Step 1: Evaluate Data Volume and Complexity

If you have 10M+ monthly hits and complex multi-channel attribution needs, then GA360's advanced features provide value. If your needs are simpler, free GA4 may suffice.

Step 2: Assess Attribution Requirements

If you need machine learning attribution models and custom attribution beyond last-click, then GA360's data-driven attribution is essential. If basic attribution works, stick with GA4.

Step 3: Consider Data Science Capabilities

If you have data scientists who need raw data access for custom analysis and machine learning, then BigQuery export justifies the cost. If not, the investment may be wasted.

Step 4: Calculate ROI on Advanced Insights

If improved attribution and insights could optimize marketing spend by more than $150K annually, then GA360 pays for itself. If marketing budget is smaller, the ROI doesn't justify the cost.

Step 5: Engage Google Sales Team

If GA360 meets your criteria, then contact Google's enterprise sales team for custom pricing and implementation planning. Expect 3-6 month evaluation and setup process.
