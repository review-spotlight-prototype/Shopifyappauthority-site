Affiliate Disclosure

Complete transparency about our affiliate relationships, commission structures, and review methodology

Our Commitment to Transparency

ShopifyAppAuthority.com is committed to providing honest, unbiased reviews and recommendations for Shopify apps and ecommerce tools. This page provides complete disclosure of our affiliate relationships, commission structures, and review methodology to ensure you have all the information needed to make informed decisions.

FTC Compliance Statement

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines and requirements, ShopifyAppAuthority.com discloses that we may earn commissions when you purchase products or services through affiliate links on our website.

This means: When you click on certain links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. These affiliate relationships help support our website operations and allow us to continue providing detailed, independent reviews and analysis.

Important Note

Our editorial opinions and review scores are never influenced by affiliate commission amounts. We maintain strict editorial independence and base all recommendations on actual testing, performance data, and objective analysis.

How We Earn Revenue

Affiliate Commissions

We participate in affiliate programs with various Shopify app developers and software companies. When you purchase through our affiliate links, we may earn a commission ranging from $10 to $500+ depending on the product and affiliate program terms.

No Pay-for-Reviews

We do not accept payment for positive reviews, guaranteed placements, or biased coverage. All reviews are based on our independent testing and analysis.

No Sponsored Content Without Disclosure

If we ever publish sponsored content (which is rare), it will be clearly labeled as "Sponsored" or "Paid Partnership" at the top of the article.

Our Review Methodology

Independent Testing Process

  1. Hands-On Experience: We have extensive experience using apps in real Shopify store environments
  2. Performance Analysis: We analyze features, pricing, and value propositions
  3. Comparative Analysis: We compare competing apps using consistent criteria and evaluation methods
  4. Cost-Benefit Analysis: We evaluate pricing relative to actual business value delivered
  5. User Experience Assessment: We evaluate setup complexity, daily usability, and support quality

Editorial Independence

Our review scores and recommendations are based solely on:

  • Our hands-on experience with app features and functionality
  • Feature completeness and quality
  • Value for money at different price points
  • Ease of implementation and use
  • Customer support quality
  • Integration quality with Shopify

Commission amounts do not influence our scores or rankings. Apps with lower commissions may rank higher than those with higher commissions if they deliver better results.

How Commission Relationships Work

Typical Commission Structure

  1. You click our affiliate link: This tags your browser with our affiliate ID
  2. You sign up for a free trial: Most companies track this as a qualified lead
  3. You upgrade to a paid plan: We receive commission only when you become a paying customer
  4. Commission is paid: Usually 30-90 days after your first payment

What This Means for You

  • No additional cost: You pay the same price whether you use our link or not
  • Same deals and promotions: You get access to all official discounts and offers
  • Same customer support: You receive the same level of service from the company
  • Full refund rights: Your refund and cancellation rights are unchanged

Maintaining Review Integrity

Our Editorial Standards

  • We use apps extensively before publishing reviews
  • We disclose both pros and cons honestly, regardless of commission potential
  • We recommend free alternatives when they provide better value
  • We update reviews regularly to reflect app changes and new features
  • We maintain detailed testing documentation for all claims

When We Don't Recommend Apps

Despite having affiliate relationships, we sometimes recommend against apps or suggest alternatives when:

  • Free alternatives provide sufficient functionality
  • The app doesn't provide good value for the price
  • Better competing solutions exist
  • The app isn't suitable for most store types
  • Setup complexity outweighs benefits for typical users
