Frequently Asked Questions

What analytics app should I install first on Shopify?

Start with Google Analytics 4 (free) for basic ecommerce tracking, then add Hotjar for user behavior insights. This combination provides comprehensive data without overwhelming complexity.

Do I need paid analytics tools if I have Google Analytics?

Yes, for deeper insights. Google Analytics shows what happened, but tools like Hotjar show how users behave, Mixpanel tracks specific events, and Kissmetrics focuses on customer lifetime value.

Which analytics tool is best for conversion optimization?

Hotjar and Crazy Egg excel at conversion optimization through heatmaps and session recordings that show exactly where users struggle on your site.

How much should I budget for analytics tools?

Start with free tools (Google Analytics 4, Hotjar free plan). Budget $50-200/month for growing stores, $300-1000/month for enterprise analytics with advanced attribution modeling.

What's the difference between attribution and analytics?

Analytics tracks what happens on your site (page views, conversions). Attribution tracks which marketing channels deserve credit for those conversions across the entire customer journey.

Should I use multiple analytics tools together?

Yes, different tools serve different purposes. Use Google Analytics for overall tracking, Hotjar for user behavior, and specialized tools like Mixpanel for event tracking or Kissmetrics for customer lifetime value.

Your Analytics & Attribution App Decision Framework

Follow this step-by-step process to choose the perfect Analytics & Attribution platform for your Shopify store

1

Establish Your Analytics Foundation

If you're just starting with analytics, then begin with Google Analytics 4 (free) for basic ecommerce tracking. This provides essential metrics without any cost and establishes your data foundation.

2

Add User Behavior Insights

If you want to optimize conversions and understand user behavior, then add Hotjar or Crazy Egg. Choose Hotjar for comprehensive insights with session recordings, or Crazy Egg for simplicity with built-in A/B testing.

3

Consider Advanced Event Tracking

If you need detailed tracking of specific user actions and product interactions, then add Mixpanel for advanced event analytics and user journey mapping. This is especially valuable for complex product catalogs.

4

Evaluate Revenue Focus Needs

If your primary goal is optimizing customer lifetime value and revenue attribution, then consider Kissmetrics for person-based analytics focused specifically on ecommerce revenue metrics and CLV optimization.

5

Scale to Enterprise Requirements

If you have enterprise-level traffic (10M+ hits/month) and need advanced attribution modeling, then upgrade to Google Analytics 360 for BigQuery integration, unlimited data processing, and advanced attribution features.

üéØ Quick Recommendation

Start with free Google Analytics 4 + Hotjar free plan for your foundation. Add Mixpanel if you need detailed event tracking, or Kissmetrics if customer lifetime value is your primary focus. Only consider GA360 if you're processing $10M+ annually and need enterprise attribution.
