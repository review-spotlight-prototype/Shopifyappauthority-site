Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best review app for Shopify stores?

Judge.me is the top choice for most Shopify stores due to its comprehensive feature set, AI-powered review requests, and excellent value with a forever free plan. It offers photo/video reviews, SEO optimization, and seamless Shopify integration.

How much do Shopify review apps typically cost?

Review apps range from free (Judge.me) to $25+/month for premium features. Most stores find success with plans between $9-25/month, depending on their review volume and required features like visual reviews, Q&A, and advanced analytics.

Do product reviews really increase conversions?

Yes, product reviews can increase conversions by 35-70%. They provide social proof, reduce purchase anxiety, and help customers make confident buying decisions. Visual reviews (photos/videos) are particularly effective at driving sales.

Can I import existing reviews to my Shopify store?

Most review apps allow importing reviews from Amazon, AliExpress, and other platforms. Apps like Loox and Judge.me make this process straightforward, helping you build social proof quickly when launching a new store.

How do I get customers to leave reviews?

Automated email sequences, incentives (discounts for photo reviews), and timing optimization are key. Send review requests 7-14 days after delivery, offer incentives for visual reviews, and make the process as simple as possible.

Should I use multiple review apps or just one?

Use one primary review app to avoid conflicts and confusion. However, you can combine on-site reviews (Judge.me/Yotpo) with external credibility platforms (Trustpilot) for comprehensive social proof across the customer journey.

Your Reviews & Social Proof App Decision Framework

Follow this step-by-step process to choose the perfect review platform for your Shopify store

1

Assess Your Current Revenue and Budget

If you're doing less than $50K/month: Start with Judge.me's free plan to build initial social proof without upfront costs.

If you're doing $50K-500K/month: Consider Judge.me paid plans or Loox for photo incentives.

If you're doing $500K+/month: Evaluate Yotpo for enterprise features or Trustpilot for independent credibility.

2

Determine Your Product Type and Visual Needs

If you sell visual products (fashion, beauty, home decor): Prioritize Okendo or Loox for photo review capabilities.

If you sell technical products or services: Focus on text reviews and Q&A functionality with Stamped.io or Yotpo.

If visual proof is crucial for conversions: Choose apps that excel at photo collection and display.

3

Evaluate Required Features and Integrations

If you need basic reviews only: Judge.me or Riviws provide excellent value for core functionality.

If you want comprehensive social proof (reviews + Q&A + NPS): Stamped.io offers the most complete solution.

If you need enterprise analytics and attribution: Yotpo provides the most advanced reporting capabilities.

4

Consider Brand Credibility Requirements

If customers Google your brand before buying: Trustpilot provides independent validation that appears in search results.

If you compete with established brands: Independent reviews on Trustpilot level the playing field.

If on-site conversion is your main focus: Stick with dedicated Shopify review apps like Judge.me or Yotpo.

5

Test and Optimize Your Choice

Start with a free trial or free plan: Test the app's interface, features, and impact on conversions.

Monitor conversion rate changes: Track how reviews impact your sales and customer behavior.

Optimize based on results: Adjust review request timing, incentives, and display options for maximum impact.

üéØ Quick Recommendation

For most Shopify stores, start with Judge.me's free plan to build initial social proof, then upgrade or switch to specialized solutions like Okendo (visual focus) or Yotpo (enterprise features) as your business grows.
